Have a Question? Check Out Our FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our services and support options below.

What is included in the Basic Plan?

The Basic Plan offers essential features for small projects, including simple development and one month of support. This is ideal for businesses starting out.

How long is the support period for each plan?

The Basic Plan includes 1 month of support, the Standard Plan offers 3 months, and the Premium Plan provides a full 6 months of support to ensure smooth operation.

Can I upgrade my plan after purchase?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time to access additional features and extended support by contacting our support team.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we maintain full transparency with our pricing. The listed prices for each plan cover all services included in that package.

How secure is the Premium Plan?

The Premium Plan includes advanced security measures such as encrypted cloud storage and regular updates to keep your application secure.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team through email or chat. Support is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM (local time).
